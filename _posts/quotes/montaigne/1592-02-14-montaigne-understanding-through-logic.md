> Who has ever acquired understanding through logic? Where are its fine promises? **Neither for living better nor for reasoning properly.**[^1]
> <cite>Michel de Montaigne [Essays III, 8]</cite>

[^1]: **Cicero, De Finibus, I, XIX.**
